# Huawei Consumer Product Theme

## Aesthetic Direction

Premium, sleek, and emotionally engaging. This theme showcases consumer devices with high-impact visuals, premium feel, and aspirational messaging.

## Reference Pages
- `consumer.huawei.com` - Main consumer site
- Product launch events - High-impact campaign style (e.g., Barcelona, Dubai launches)

## Visual Characteristics

### Overall Feel
- Luxurious yet approachable
- Showcases product craftsmanship
- Emotional and aspirational
- High production value
- For launch events: More dramatic, event-feel, countdown elements

### Color Treatment
- **Light variant**: White `#FFFFFF` to off-white `#FAFAFA` backgrounds
- **Dark variant**: Deep blacks `#0D0D0D` to dark gray `#1A1A1A`
- **Primary accent**: Huawei Red `#CF0A2C` for CTAs and highlights
- **Premium accent**: Gold `#C9A227` for flagship products
- Use product colors prominently in hero sections

### Typography Feel
- **Headings**: Bold, modern, slightly condensed for impact
- **Taglines**: Elegant, emotional, descriptive
- Large display sizes for hero text
- Clear hierarchy from headline to body

### Layout Approach
- Full-bleed hero sections (90% viewport height)
- Large product imagery (hero product should be prominent)
- Alternating feature blocks (text left/image right, then reversed)
- Generous negative space
- Smooth transitions between sections
- For launch events: More cinematic, less structured grid

## Key Design Elements

### Hero Section
- Full viewport height or near-full
- Product as visual centerpiece
- Floating product animation (subtle up/down motion)
- Gradient overlays on dark backgrounds
- Clear primary CTA ("Buy Now" or "Learn More")
- Quick spec highlights (3-4 key features)
- For launch: Video backgrounds, countdown timers, event branding

### Product Imagery
- Large, high-quality product photos
- Clean white or gradient backgrounds
- Multiple angles or lifestyle shots
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Reflection effects for premium products

### Feature Sections
- Alternating layout for visual interest
- Large imagery with concise text
- Feature highlights with icons or numbers
- Seamless transitions between sections

### Color/Variant Selector
- Circular color swatches
- Selected state with ring indicator
- Smooth transition when changing colors

### Pricing & CTA
- Prominent price display
- Clear "Buy Now" / "Pre-order" buttons
- Secondary "Compare" or "Learn More" option

### Product Cards (Listings)
- White cards with generous padding
- Large product image (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Clean product name and price
- Hover: lift effect with enhanced shadow

## Launch Event Variants

### Event Hero
- Full-screen video background
- Event branding/date prominently displayed
- Countdown timer for product reveal
- "Live" or "Replay" indicators
- Social media integration

### Product Reveal Section
- Dramatic unveil animation
- Product name reveals with impact
- Key specs appear sequentially
- Emotional storytelling elements

### Event Recap
- Gallery of product shots
- Key moments highlight
- Quote/testimonial sections
- Related product links

## Imagery Style
- High-resolution product photography
- Lifestyle imagery showing products in use
- Premium, aspirational settings
- Show material details and craftsmanship

## Dark Mode Aesthetic
- Deep blacks and dark grays
- White/light text for contrast
- Subtle gradients for depth
- Accent colors pop more intensely

## Animations
- Elegant entrance animations (fade up)
- Floating product effect
- Smooth hover transitions
- Scroll-triggered reveals
- Staggered content appearance
- For launch: More dramatic reveal animations

## Responsive Behavior
- Full-width product images on mobile
- Sticky CTA bar on mobile
- Maintain premium feel at all sizes

## Premium Product Handling
- Use dark backgrounds for flagship/ultimate products
- Gold accents for premium tier
- More dramatic imagery
- Larger display typography
